Introduction

World War 2 was an important part of history. It started when Adolf Hitler (a former German soldier in World War 1) was upset since the Allies won WW1. Which meant
that Germany had to reduce their millitary to only 100,000 soldiers (which was not that much) and they couldn't have an air force.
Germany also lost some land, and they had to pay a lot of money that they didn't have. Hitler said to the people of Germany that he could fix it
all, so he started gaining folowers.

In 1923, he attempted a march in Munich with his followers but he got arrested. His popularity kept on growing though. In 1933, the President
made him Chancellor and he believed that he was Germany's destined saviour, establishing a dictatorship. Then there was Benito Mussolini in Italy
who was a lot like Hitler.
They had the same enemies, became friends and became an alliance. Hitler also became an ally with Japan too. They became the Axis alliance.

Hitler disobeyed the Allied forces (U.S., Britain, France, Canada, etc. ) when he invaded Poland starting World War 2. D-Day and the Battle of the Bulge were two big and famous
battles in WW2. To get more info about World War 2 go to my other pages at the top in the menu.
